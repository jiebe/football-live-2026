Privacy Policy

Football Live 2026 · Last updated: 12 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Football Live 2026 ("the app", "we", "us") handles information when you use the app. We have designed the app to require as little of your data as possible.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information about you. The app does not require an account, a login, or any registration. We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or location.

Third-Party Services

To show live scores, fixtures, and standings, the app retrieves data from third-party sports data providers over the internet. When the app makes these requests, your device's IP address is necessarily shared with those providers as a normal part of any internet connection. We do not control how those providers handle this technical information, and we encourage you to review the privacy practices of the services your data passes through. We do not combine this with any other information, and we do not use it to identify you.

Analytics and Advertising

The app does not use analytics tools, advertising networks, or third-party tracking technologies. We do not track your activity across other apps or websites.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children. Because we collect no personal data, the app does not direct any data collection at children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your country).

Data Retention

Because we do not collect personal data, we do not retain any personal data about you.

Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights over your personal data under laws such as the EU/UK General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) or the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data for us to access, correct, or delete. If you have questions about this, you can contact us using the details below.

Changes to This Policy

If we change how the app handles data in the future — for example, by adding features that collect information — we will update this policy and revise the date at the top before those changes take effect.
