Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an account to use the app?
No. The app works straight away with no sign-up, login, or registration required.

Why is a score or fixture wrong or delayed?
Live scores and fixtures are supplied by a third-party data provider. Occasionally updates can lag behind the live match by a short time, or a result may be corrected after the fact. If you spot a persistent error, email us and we'll look into it.

The app isn't loading scores.
The app needs an internet connection to fetch live data. Check that you're connected to Wi-Fi or mobile data, then close and reopen the app. If the problem continues, let us know which device and iOS version you're using.

How do I report a bug or suggest a feature?
Email us at the address above with as much detail as you can — what you did, what you expected, and what happened. Suggestions are very welcome.

What data does the app collect?
None. The app does not collect personal information. You can read the full details in our Privacy Policy.
